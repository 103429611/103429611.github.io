Who Are We?

InfraWatch is an organisation specialising in the development and deployment of smart AI camera systems for local and federal governments. Our technology monitors road and energy infrastructure in real time, providing governments with the data they need to reduce risks, guide future developments, and make smarter strategic planning decisions. With the hardware and expertise to support a state-wide rollout, we are ready to work with your government to keep communities running safer and more efficiently.
